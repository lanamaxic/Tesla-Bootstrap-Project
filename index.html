Choose your vehicle

All vehicles come with dual-motor all-wheel drive, premium interior and audio system.

The control is digital and therefore much more precise than with mechanical power transmission, which guarantees better handling and superior traction under all conditions.
Your vehicle can also be easily powered by just one engine, so you don't have to worry about getting stuck. If one engine fails, you can safely reach your destination with the second one.
The Tesla all-wheel drive uses two separately controlled electric motors. In contrast to conventional all-wheel drive, these two motors control the torque on the front and rear wheels separately.
